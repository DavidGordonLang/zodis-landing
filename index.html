What Žodis helps you do

Learn real Lithuanian — not textbook Lithuanian

Žodis focuses on phrases people actually use in Lithuania: the way partners speak to each other, the way friends joke, the way shop assistants greet you, and the way people naturally phrase things in daily life.

Every phrase comes with clear Lithuanian audio and pronunciation guidance, so you learn tone, emphasis, and rhythm — something Google Translate simply doesn't deliver.

Built for expats, partners, and newcomers

Whether you're here for work, studying, moving in with Lithuanian family, or just trying to feel more confident in conversations, Žodis adapts to your needs. Choose who you're speaking to (neutral, male, female) and the tone you want (friendly, neutral, polite).

The app shapes the Lithuanian for you automatically.

Create your own personalised phrasebook

Every phrase you translate can be saved with one tap. Organise your library with categories like Phrases, Questions, Words, and Numbers — just like inside the app.

Your phrasebook grows with you, becoming your personal Lithuanian toolkit.

Speak with clarity — even as a beginner

Play Lithuanian audio at normal or slow speeds. Perfect for learning tone and rhythm.

Each phrase includes literal and natural English translations so you understand how Lithuanians think.
