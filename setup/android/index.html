Install Žodis on Android

This installs Žodis like a real app (icon on your Home Screen, full-screen, quicker access).

Open the app Back to Žodis home

Step-by-step

  1. Open the app in Chrome: learning-lithuanian.vercel.app
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right).
  3. Tap “Add to Home screen” (or sometimes “Install app”).
  4. Confirm. You’ll now have a Žodis icon on your Home Screen.
  5. Launch it from the Home Screen for the best experience (full-screen).

Quick first-run checklist

  • Sign in with Google so your library syncs across devices.
  • Try a translation in Home, then save it to your Library.
  • In Settings, check your voice/audio options if something sounds off.

Common hiccups

I can’t see “Add to Home screen”.
Make sure you’re using Chrome (not an in-app browser), and you’re on the main app URL.

Audio doesn’t play.
Some Android setups require you to tap once in the app before audio starts reliably. Try playing again after your first interaction.
