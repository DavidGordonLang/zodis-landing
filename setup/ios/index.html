Install Žodis on iPhone

iOS needs one specific step: you must add it from Safari.

Open the app Back to Žodis home

Step-by-step (Safari)

  1. Open the app in Safari: learning-lithuanian.vercel.app
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with an arrow pointing up).
  3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
  4. Confirm the name (Žodis) and tap Add.
  5. Launch Žodis from the Home Screen icon (this is the “app-like” version).

Quick first-run checklist

  • Sign in with Google so your library syncs across devices.
  • Try a translation in Home, then save it to your Library.
  • In Settings, check voice/audio options if something sounds off.

Common iPhone hiccups

It doesn’t feel like an app.
Make sure you launched it from the Home Screen icon, not from Safari tabs.

“Add to Home Screen” isn’t showing.
You’re probably in an in-app browser (Instagram, Gmail, etc). Copy the link and open it in Safari directly.
